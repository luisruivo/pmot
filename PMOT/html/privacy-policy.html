Privacy Policy

We are aware of the importance of the treatment of personal data. In this policy we explain what the personal data you provide is used for.

This information applies to anyone who has accessed this website or is a current or past client of Peaceful Mind Online Therapy. The relevance of the information found herein will vary, depending on whether or not you are receiving Peaceful Mind Online Therapy’s services.

Who We Are
  • Holder: Peacefulmindonlinetherapy – 12591359
  • Register: CBT Register UK
  • Email address: info@peacefulmindonlinetherapy.net
  • Website: Peacefulmindonlinetherapy.net

Peaceful mind online therapy will collect and use such information only as needed and as is relevant to the provision of therapy or consultation services as requested by you. Examples of the information collected and why, is described below.

Your personal information may be used by Peaceful mind online therapy to provide therapy services or information to you, to respond to phone calls, emails or provide resources to augment your therapy experience.

What Personal Data We Collect
Contact Form

Peaceful mind online therapy has an encrypted contact form. This form requests your name and email address so that we can contact you to arrange a time for your free consultation. We return requests for consultations via email or phone call.

Intake

At the time of the initial email we collect:

Your name, address, phone number and email address.

This is to ensure that you are the person who is seeking therapy and we can contact with you outside of the session if needed, during an emergency or for therapy.

Therapy

Any information you choose to share about your personal situation, past or present is for the purpose of providing therapy and collaborating with you on a treatment plan.

Email

Email is not considered a secure and confidential way of communicating. For the protection of your privacy, please do not email information you would not be comfortable discussing in a public space. Peaceful Mind Online Therapy is happy to confidentially discuss your concerns either in session, or during your free consultation.

Cookies

If you leave a comment on our site you may opt-in to saving your name and email address using cookies. These are for your convenience so that you do not have to fill in your details again should you leave another comment. Cookies last for one year.

If you visit our login page, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

When you log in, we will also set up several cookies to save your login information and your screen display choices. Login cookies last for two days, and screen options cookies last for a year. If you select «Remember Me», your login will last for two weeks. If you log out of your account, the login cookies will be removed.

Who We Share Your Data With

Your identity, attendance and all personal information communicated by you in therapy is strictly confidential, with the following exceptions:

  • You have expressed the desire to harm yourself or someone else.
  • In situations of suspected or known child abuse/neglect or suspected elder abuse or neglect, reporting is required by law; failure to do so could result in harm to the child or adult in question, in addition to professional penalties such as fines, jail time and the removal of licence to practice.
  • Should the therapy record be subpoenaed by the court.
  • If Peaceful mind online therapy is requesting clinical consultation or supervision; no identifying information about you is released.
  • Should Peaceful Mind Online Therapy be required to retain the services of a collection agency or seek legal counsel.
  • Should Peaceful Mind Online Therapy need to retain the services of a bookkeeper who handles copies of receipts.
  • Should public health officials require the disclosure of names of people with whom Peaceful mind online therapy has had recent contact.
Disclosure To Third Parties

Peaceful Mind Online Therapy is not authorised to disclose your personal information to any third party without your express written and signed consent. Peaceful mind online therapy cannot respond to requests from concerned family members or friends about clients’ attendance, disclosure in session or the treatment plan; please talk to your loved ones directly. This also applies to family members or friends who are paying for the client’s sessions.

There are restrictions, albeit rare, to confidentiality which involved disclosure to third parties; these are detailed in the above section, “Confidentiality.”

Protection Of Your Personal Information

Peaceful Mind Online Therapy treats all aspects of your personal information with the greatest care and takes active steps to protect your information.

Peaceful Mind Online Therapy also provides secure video, audio or typed therapy through the online platform Zoom. Sessions are anonymous and are not recorded or stored.

Any email exchanges are also part of the personal record.

Clients have a right to view or receive a copy of their file, or portions of the file.

How Long We Retain Your Data

If you leave a comment on the website, the comment and its metadata are retained indefinitely. This is so we can recognise and approve any follow-up comments automatically instead of holding them in a moderation queue.

What Rights You Have Over Your Data

You can request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where We Send Your Data

Visitor comments may be checked through an automated spam detection service.

Privacy Policy Changes

The information above is subject to periodic revision and changes. The current version will always be posted at this page on peacefulmindonlinetherapy.net. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact Peaceful Mind Online Therapy directly through one of the means noted above.

I invite you to explore my website and contact me with any questions. A call today will begin a journey toward meaningful, long-lasting change.
