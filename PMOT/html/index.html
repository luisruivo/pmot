Why Choose Online Therapy?
Flexible Hours
Choose a time for therapy that fits your personal schedule.
Quality Service
Studies have shown that online CBT is equal to and often more effective than face to face therapy (e.g. Luo et al, 2020 and also Gratzer & Khalid-Khan, 2016.)
Security Ensured
Abi is a BABCP fully accredited therapist. Sessions are done using a secure, encrypted platform.
Be Yourself
Often it feels easier to share personal information when therapy is online and you are in a familiar and relaxed atmosphere.
Flexible Contact
Choose the type of contact that you feel most comfortable with. This could be a video call, phone call or even chat.
Flexible Location
Access therapy from the most convenient and comfortable place for you. This could be your work office or own home.

A Few Words About Me
Abi Brayford, PGDip
I am a qualified Cognitive Behavioural Therapist who is accredited with the BABCP. The BABCP is the national governing body for CBT Therapists. I received my undergraduate degree from Lancaster University with a Bachelors degree in Psychology. I then went on to earn a Certificate in Psychological Therapies from Surrey University and a Post Graduate Diploma in Cognitive Behavioural Therapy from the University of Manchester.
