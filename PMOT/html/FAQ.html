Frequently Asked Questions
What do I need to get started?
You will need a smart phone, desktop computer, laptop or tablet and a steady internet connection. You will also need a built-in camera and microphone. That’s it!
How to enter the platform?
Download zoom here. I will send you a link to join the appointment via email just before our planned session time. Click on the link which will take you to our session.
Payment and fees
How do I pay for sessions?
All sessions are paid for via paypal- a system used world wide by businesses and companies to pay safely online. You can set up an account here. Just add your debit or credit card details- this takes 5 minutes.
When do I pay for sessions?
Full payment must be made before the start of the session. A time must be agreed by both parties before payment is sent. You will receive confirmation of payment via email.

Peaceful Mind Online Therapy is a confidential service. Any information shared will remain completely confidential.
