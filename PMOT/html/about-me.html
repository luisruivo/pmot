A Few Words About Me
Abi Brayford, PGDip
If you’re anything like me I felt very overwhelmed the first time I made the decision to access therapy around 12 years ago. What type is best for me? What am I hoping to get from this? What if my therapist doesn’t understand me? These are all perfectly natural concerns and very important questions to explore, which is why I offer an initial consultation that is free of charge. This can be done via call, video, email or text. You should feel informed and confident when starting therapy that you have chosen the best option for your needs.
My Way Of Working
After starting therapy I then went on to have the most profound and life changing experience of my life with my therapist. I decided from that day forward that I wanted do the same for others. I spent the next ten years training, studying and working in various forms of therapy to help me to connect with and understand each client and the difficulties that they bring to our sessions. After obtaining my Bachelors degree in psychology and since starting my work in mental health in the NHS ten years ago, I have gone onto gain qualifications in counselling, psychological therapies and cognitive behavioural therapy. I am fully accredited with the BABCP which is the official therapy governing body for the UK. You can find me by searching my name ‘Abigail Brayford’ using the following link: (CBT Register UK) Although I am an expert in CBT, you are the expert of your life and your experiences so it is essential for us to work together in order for you to achieve the changes in your life that matter the most to you.
